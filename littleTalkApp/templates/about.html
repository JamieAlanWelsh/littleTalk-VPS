Who we are

We’re a small, mission-driven team with expertise in speech and language therapy, education, and AI-driven software development.

Our platform, Chatterdillo, is designed to be fun for children, intuitive for teachers, and powerful enough to make impact, especially for pupils who often fall through the cracks.

What we do

Chatterdillo offers gamified, curriculum-aligned speech and language exercises for use in school and at home.

It’s more than just content, it’s a smart system that helps teachers know what to do, when, and why.

Our Story

Hi, I’m Amy! I’m a Licensed Speech and Language Therapist and co-founder of Chatterdillo.

In schools, it’s tough when a child is struggling to communicate and staff aren’t sure where to begin, or are left waiting for external support.

I first saw this while working as a one-to-one assistant supporting a child with Down’s Syndrome. I was given a pack of exercises but no guidance and even with my training, I felt unsure. It showed me how overwhelming it can be for staff who want to help but don’t have the right tools.

That’s why we created Chatterdillo: to empower educators with accessible, evidence-based resources that make it easy to build children’s speech and language skills in the classroom, with confidence and clarity.
