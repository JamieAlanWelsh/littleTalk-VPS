Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who is this tool for?

A: This tool is for parents of children with speech and language needs, the tool can assist speech and language development through routine exercises.

Q: How should the app be used?

A: The app should be used in collaboration with your child. Add a "learner" in the profile section to get started. We will suggest exercises for your child, we only ask that you practise daily and supervise your child's learning with positive encouragement.

Q: Do I really practise daily?

A: Research shows that frequent learning is three times more likely to lead to improvement in results. For this reason, we encourage users to practise daily, however, we understand that life can be busy, so please feel free to practise whenever it suits you and your child - it all counts!

Q: What is Colourful Semantics?

A: Colourful Semantics is a tool that aims to help children understand sentence structure and language through the use of a colour-coded system. This system helps children break down sentences and visually see what aspects of a sentence need to be present.

  • Understand the role of different parts of a sentence
  • Develop a wider vocabulary
  • Improve their narrative skills
  • Improve their ability to answer questions
  • Improve their written language (literacy skills)

Q: Is this a replacement for speech therapy?

A: This tool is intended to be a supplementary/complementary tool to accelerate speech and language development. The team at LittleTalk encourages anyone with speech and language needs to continue seeking traditional speech therapy services in addition to using our service.

Q: How do I add a new learner?

A: To add a new learner, go to your profile page and click the "Add Learner" button.

Q: How do I remove a learner?

A: Select the learner you want to remove, click "edit learner", click "remove learner", bare in mind that this decision is permanent.

Q: Can I track progress for multiple learners?

A: Yes, you can manage multiple learners and track their progress from your profile page.
