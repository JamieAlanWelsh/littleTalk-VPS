Speech and Language Support That Saves Your School Time, Budget and Prep

LittleTalk is a web app designed by Speech and Language Therapists to help schools provide speech and language support for children aged 2-11.

Our app recommends evidence-based exercises based on an in-app assessment, enabling staff to deliver high-quality SEN interventions directly through the platform - no specialist training or heavy prep required.
