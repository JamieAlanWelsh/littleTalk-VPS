Welcome to LittleTalk! 👋

LittleTalk is a supplementary tool for speech and language development that offers a variety of high-quality, research-backed exercises, with built-in progress tracking and adjustable learning levels.

We will recommend exercises, we only ask that you show up for 10-15 minutes daily to see results.

Access 🚀

We believe high-quality speech development exercises should be easily accessible to anyone who needs them.

Our mission is to empower parents to conveniently practice speech development with their learner in multiple settings.

Increased accessibility encourages consistent carryover, allowing learners to develop and apply their functional language in a variety of daily environments.

Supported, Engaging Learning 👩‍🏫

LittleTalk is adaptable to your learner's unique learning journey, providing the perfect scaffold to support their language development.

Take advantage of a range of learning levels and a wide variety of activities tailored to different skill levels.

Get excited about practice by earning exp and rewards, ensuring your learner stays motivated while building their speech and language skills at their own pace.
