About LittleTalk

Welcome to LittleTalk

LittleTalk is a tool for speech and language therapy that offers a variety of high quality Speech Therapy exercises, with built in progress tracking and adjustable learning levels.

Get started by selecting an exercise on the left sidebar or reading more about our mission below.

Access 🚀

We believe high quality speech therapy exercises should be easily accessible to anyone who needs them from any device.

Our mission is to empower parents, children and education practitioners to conveniently practice speech therapy in multiple settings.

Increased accessibility encourages consistent carryover, allowing children to develop and apply their functional language in a variety daily environments.

Supported Learning 👩‍🏫

Little Talk is adaptable to your child's unique learning journey, with customisable difficulty levels and diverse settings, providing the perfect scaffold to support their language development.

We aim to make learning more engaging and effective by including a wide variety of activities tailored to different skill levels, ensuring your child stays motivated while building their speech and language skills at their own pace.

Data 📊

Utilise user performance data to assist with targeted learning, adaptive progression and help researchers to discover valuable insights that might further our understanding of language disorders.
