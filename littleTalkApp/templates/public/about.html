What is Chatterdillo?

At Chatterdillo, we believe that speech and language support shouldn’t be a postcode lottery — or something only accessible to those with the loudest needs.

Who we are

We’re a small, mission-driven team with expertise in speech and language therapy, education, and software development.

Our platform, Chatterdillo, is designed to be fun for children, intuitive for teachers, and powerful enough to make impact, especially for pupils who often fall through the cracks.

What we do

Chatterdillo offers gamified, curriculum-aligned speech and language exercises for use in school and at home.

It’s more than just content, it’s a smart system that helps teachers know what to do, when, and why.

Our impact

Our pilot schools are already seeing promising results. With Chatterdillo, schools can support 15-30 more pupils per site each year who would otherwise go without structured help.

Because our system is low-prep and high-frequency, we estimate learning progress could triple, especially when reinforced at home.
